Welcome to Our School!

Explore our courses and programs designed to help you or your child to succeed.

──────────────────────── Courses We Offer ────────────────────────

CASA

Our CASA program (3-5 Years Old) is designed for young learners to develop foundational skills in a nurturing environment. Through hands-on activities and interactive lessons, children are encouraged to explore their creativity, build social skills, and foster a love for learning from an early age.

Primary Grade School

Our Primary Grade School program (6-8 Years Old) focuses on building a strong academic foundation while nurturing critical thinking and problem-solving skills. Students engage in a variety of subjects, including language arts, mathematics, science, and social studies, through interactive lessons and collaborative projects that promote a love for learning.

Intermediate Grade School

Our Intermediate Grade School program (9-12 Years Old) is designed to challenge students academically while fostering personal growth and social development. Through a comprehensive curriculum that includes advanced subjects and extracurricular activities, students are encouraged to explore their interests, develop leadership skills, and prepare for future academic success.

Junior High School

Our Junior High School program (13-16 Years Old) is designed to provide students with a well-rounded education that prepares them for the challenges of high school and beyond. Through a rigorous curriculum that includes core subjects, electives, and extracurricular activities, students are encouraged to develop critical thinking skills, explore their interests, and build a strong foundation for future academic and personal success.

Senior High School

Our Senior High School program (17-18 Years Old) is designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary for success in higher education and future careers. With a focus on specialized tracks such as STEM, Humanities, and Arts, students have the opportunity to explore their passions while developing critical thinking, problem-solving, and leadership skills through a combination of academic coursework and practical experiences.
