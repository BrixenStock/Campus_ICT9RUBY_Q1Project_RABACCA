──────────────────────── O.B. Montessori in action ────────────────────────

Child-Led Learning

At OB Montessori Center, children are given the freedom to explore lessons and activities based on their interests and readiness. Instead of being rushed or forced to move at the same pace as others, students learn at a speed that is comfortable for them. This approach helps children develop independence, self-discipline, and confidence, while allowing teachers to guide and support each child’s individual learning journey.

Critical Thinking

Food Fair Our Annual Food Fair is an event where students showcase their culinary skills and creativity. They plan, prepare, and present a variety of dishes, learning about nutrition, teamwork, and entrepreneurship in the process.

Global & Ethical Perspectives

House of Sarang in Taguig City Through OBMC'S Peace Projects, Students are taught to have empathy and help those in need. For example, our students have visited the House of Sarang, an orphanage in Taguig City, to spend time with the children there and provide them with support and care. Spending their time playing games, reading stories, and sharing meals with the children.

Small Class, Strong Community

Student Council Activities Our student council organizes various activities and events that promote school spirit and community involvement. These activities include charity drives, cultural celebrations, and school-wide competitions. Through these events, students learn the importance of teamwork, leadership, and giving back to the community.
